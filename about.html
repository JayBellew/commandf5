Hey I'm Jay Bellew (Bell-Yoo)!

I've been a UI/UX designer for over 12 years and I finally listened to my first interface about 18 months ago while reviewing one of my sites for accessibility problems.

That first day tabbing throught the UI, with my heaphones on, changed the way I approach design

I found that I wasn't alone; many highly skilled and well intentioned designers and developers never really think to listen to their interface to truly put themselves in the shoes of their disabled users.

I created Command F5 as a way to force myself to learn and improve

At some point I hope to grow the site into a reliable resource for accessible design and development. Until that point, I'll be making mistakes and building!
