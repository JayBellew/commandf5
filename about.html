Hey I'm Jay Bellew (Bell-Yoo)!

I've been a UI/UX designer for over 12 years and I finally listened to my first interface a few years ago while reviewing one of my sites for accessibility. That first day tabbing through the UI, with my headphones on, changed the way I approach design.

I created Command F5 as a way to challenge myself to learn and improve. At some point I hope to grow the site into a reliable resource for accessible design and development. Until that point, I'll be making mistakes and building!
